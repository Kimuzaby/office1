The Microsoft Office 365 course called "Office Ready!" is an initiative that arises from the need to learn about Office tools in their 2016 and 2019 versions. This time, expanding our horizons and reaching the Cantón Veracruz School Center.

This first edition of the course took place during the months of May, June, and July of the year 2023, with 157 enrolled students and a total of 131 who successfully completed the course. We made use of the computers provided by the Ministry of Education and the Office package that comes integrated with them, as well as the computer facilities offered by the Cantón Veracruz School Center. This was made possible thanks to the management and support of the director, Agustín Hernández.
